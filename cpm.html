CPM Meter

CPM Meter allows you to measure your Clicks Per Minute, aka "how many times can I click during a minute". It uses an accurate method of measuring your keypresses and the time. The CPM is real-time, and has no time limit.To begin, start clicking, or click the Start button.
If you want to stop, click the stop button. When stopped, you can restart the meter by pressing a key, or by clicking the Start button again.
Hint: You can hit multiple keys at once.
